Reduction of Bias in Likelihood Ratio Tests for Structural Equation Modelling

Mahesh Solanki

This paper presents a computational framework for reducing systematic bias in likelihood ratio tests within structural equation modelling. We develop Bartlett Correction methods that achieve 23% reduction in Type I error rates across simulation studies, with applications in psychology and healthcare analytics.

Causal Discovery in High-Dimensional Observational Data: A Neural-Symbolic Approach

Mahesh Solanki

We propose a neural-symbolic framework for causal discovery that combines the scalability of deep learning with the interpretability of symbolic reasoning. Our method achieves state-of-the-art performance on benchmark datasets while providing interpretable causal graphs.
